Petron

One of the successful gasoline station

Here's the time line achievements of Petron Gasoline Station:

  • 1933 - Established as Esso Standard Eastern, Inc., the company started its operations in the Philippines.
  • 1966 - Rebranded as Petron Corporation after the government acquired the Esso Standard Eastern, Inc. assets.
  • 1994 - Petron becomes the first oil company in the Philippines to be listed on the Philippine Stock Exchange.
  • 2004 - The company inaugurated the Petron Bataan Refinery, one of the most advanced in the Asia-Pacific region.
  • 2010 - Opened the Petron Lakbay Alalay program, providing assistance to motorists during peak travel seasons.
  • 2013 - Launched the Petron TriAction Performance Gasoline, designed to improve engine efficiency.
  • 2014 - Petron completes its $2 billion Refinery Master Plan, enhancing production capacity and efficiency.
  • 2016 - Introduced the Petron Blaze 100 Euro 6, an advanced and environment-friendly gasoline product.
  • 2018 - Petron ranked as the Philippines' top corporation by Business World's Top 1000 Corporations.
  • 2020 - Despite global challenges, Petron maintained its commitment to quality and sustainability.
